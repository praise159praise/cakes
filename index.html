About Us
Baker, chef; artist, teacher. Priscilla Asein, founder of Prisca’s Cakes & Kitchen, is a renowned expert in providing catering services with a sense of timeliness, order and satisfaction. Over 24 years, she has educated herself on quality cooking, excellent cake design and top-notch event catering. Through the Prisca’s academy, she wishes to pass on these skills to home and professional cooks.
